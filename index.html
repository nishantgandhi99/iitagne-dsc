Event FAQs

What is the price of the ticket ?

The cost to enter venue is $5 for students and $10 for others. You pay $10 extra to participate in competition.

What is included in my ticket ?

With Venue Entry ticket:

You can attend expert lectures and data science training program.

With Participation ticket:

You can attend expert lectures and data science training program. You can take participation in data science challenge competition.

How Data Science Competition & Speaker Series is aligned in event?

The Expert Speaker Series and Data Science Competition will progress in parallel. Those who are not taking part in competition can attend expert talks. The competitors can also take a break while working on solution to attend expert talks.
